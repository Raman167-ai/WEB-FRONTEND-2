Join the Siem Reap Hidden Treasures Small-Group Tour to explore Koh Ker, Beng Mealea, and Banteay Srei. Discover the remote ruins of the ancient capital of Koh Ker, wander through the jungle-clad Beng Mealea, and marvel at the intricately carved Banteay Srei temple. With an expert guide, uncover these lesser-visited archaeological wonders beyond Angkor and gain an authentic perspective on Siem Reap's hidden treasures.

Itinerary

Hotel Pick-up Included

Depart your hotel between 7:30-8:00 am for a full day tour of Banteay Srei, Koh Ker and Beng Mealea Temple. Please be ready at the hotel lobby by 7:30 am.

Experience the life-style of Countryside

Explore the heart of rural Cambodia, witnessing locals in their daily routines and admiring picturesque rice paddies and traditional homes. Visit the Palm Sugar Village to discover the art of homemade Cambodian sugar production for families and communities.

Visit the Lady Temple of Banteay Srei

Witness the exquisitely carved and well-preserved Banteay Srei temple. Its intricate sandstone reliefs are considered the finest and most skillfully crafted in Cambodia.

Discover an Abandoned Capital of Cambodia

Explore the Koh Ker archaeological site and ancient Khmer city Chok Gargyar. Visit three remarkable temples in Koh Ker, including the iconic Prasat Thom pyramid, Prasat Kroes Linga, and Prasat Prum. Enjoy a local lunch before your next exciting adventure.

 

Visit Titanic Temple of Beng Mealea

Discover the enchanting Beng Mealea, a 12th-century jungle-shrouded temple, after a local lunch. Explore the lush, overgrown ruins, captivating visitors with their untouched, centuries-old charm despite lacking full restoration.

Return Back to Siem Reap

Transfer you back to the Siem Reap downtown and it is possible to drop you off, either at your hotel or Old market area. You are supposed to be back to your hotel by 17:00.
