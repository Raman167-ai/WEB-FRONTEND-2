About Golden Corn Restaurant

Our vision

We're passionate about creating a new dining experience and work tirelessly to make it a reality. Our goal is to make every interaction with our guests extraordinary, which drives us to constantly innovate and push culinary boundaries.

Story of our commitment

Golden Corn Restaurant is a family-run establishment in Siem Reap town, behind Park Hyatt Hotel on Taphul Road 021. Since 2010, we have been delighting locals and tourists with delicious Khmer cuisine. In early 2022, we proudly opened another restaurant in Phnom Penh to better cater to the market. What sets us apart is our unique incorporation of pumpkin into our dishes, making Golden Pumpkin a household name.

Corn is a nutrient-rich vegetable that is packed with vitamins and minerals, making it a healthy addition to any diet. It is particularly good for heart health and can help to lower cholesterol levels. In Cambodia, pumpkin has long been a favorite ingredient in many sweet desserts, and Golden pumpkin has found creative ways to incorporate it into savory dishes as well.

Indulge in a delicious meal at Golden Corn restaurant, situated only minutes away from top tourist spots such as the Russian market, Cambodia National Genocidal Museum, Royal palace, and Wat Phnom Daun Penh. With nearby accommodations, supermarkets, banks, and currency exchange centers, your dining experience is sure to be hassle-free.

Awards & recognitions

One of the best restaurants in Cambodia, known for offering quality Khmer and Western cuisine. Our aim is to always keep our guests happy, which has contributed to the enduring fame of Golden Pumpkin until today.
