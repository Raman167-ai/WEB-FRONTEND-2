Golden Corn Restaurant

A wonderful place to have brunch, lunch, and dinner with friends and family

Corn is a nutrient-rich vegetable that is packed with vitamins and minerals, making it a healthy addition to any diet. It is particularly good for heart health and can help to lower cholesterol levels. In Cambodia, Corn has long been a favorite ingredient in many sweet desserts, and Golden Corn has found creative ways to incorporate it into savory dishes as well.

"Indulge in a delicious meal at Golden Corn restaurant, situated only minutes away from top tourist spots such as the Russian market, Cambodia National Genocidal Museum, Royal palace, and Wat Phnom Daun Penh. With nearby accommodations, supermarkets, banks, and currency exchange centers, your dining experience is sure to be hassle-free."

  • No MSG (Monosodium Glutamate) or artificial flavouring
  • Fresh ingredients sourced daily from local and organic farms
  • Healthy and tasty dishes for whole family
