TOUR EXPERIENCE

Tour Start - 8.00am (flexible time)

Morning: recommend to start 8.00am (flexible time) after having breakfast, pick up from your hotel in Siem Reap and travel by A/C vehicle to purchase the temples ticket. Visit the Southern Gate of Angkor Thom, flanked by a row of 54 stone figures on each side – gods to the left and demons to the right – and explore the fortified city of Angkor Thom. Marvel at the beautifully crafted central towers of The Bayon (12th century), adorned with more than 200 enormous faces.

Next, highlight by the famous Baphuon, Phimeanakas, Terrace of the Leper King and the Terrace of Elephants on your way to the stunning Temple.

Enjoy a short rest and an optional lunch meal at the restaurant and the temple (your own payment).

Continue your aternoon with a visit to the atmospheric Ta Prohm temple, stands as one of the most magical places in Angkor Complex. The sight of massive tree roots hugging old stones creates a scene straight from a fairytale, gives visitors a chance to see how nature and human works can blend in stunning ways.

Spend about two hours exploring Angkor Wat complex and the interior of this magnificent temple. Spend about two hours exploring Angkor Wat complex and the interior of this magnificent temple, looking to the countless stories depicted in the bas-relief carvings, providing insights into life during the height of the Khmer Empire.

Enjoy the beautifull atmosphere of Phnom Bakheng, that is a popular spot to witness Sunset due to its elevated position, offering panoramic views of Angkor Wat and the surrounding Angkor Archaeological Park. The temple’s 360-degree vista provides an unparalleled opportunity to observe the sun setting over the ancient Khmer architecture and lush landscapes, creating a breathtaking spectacle, Then returning to your hotel.

Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of the Angkor Archaeological Park before returning to your hotel.
