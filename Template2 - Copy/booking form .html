Itinerary

Hotel Pick-up included

Depart your hotel between 8:00-8:30 am to discover the birth place of Khmer Empire, Phnom Kulen Mountan. Please be ready at the hotel lobby by 8:00 am.

Experience Siem Reap's Countryside

Explore rural Cambodian villages, witnessing local life in rice paddies and traditional homes. Visit Phum Preah Dak to learn about and see the making of palm cake and sugar.

Poeng Ta Kho (Amazing Cliff) at Kulen Mountain

Commanding views from Kulen's dramatic cliff summits while endless emerald jungles and mystical mountain peaks unfold below. Experience adventure redefined through our intimate group explorations of Cambodia's most breathtaking natural amphitheater.

Visit Wat Preah Ang Thom Pagoda

Unlock the mysteries of Cambodia's sacred heritage with knowledgeable native guides! Explore revered Buddhist monasteries showcasing serene reclining Buddha figures and elaborate artistic ceiling displays. Discover how spiritual devotion and natural beauty intertwine in these timeless holy places.

River of a Thousand Lingas Kulen Mountain

Discover the legendary Sacred River carved with ancient Hindu symbols from the 9th century, alongside cascading multi-level falls ideal for refreshing swims and riverside dining. This hallowed ground witnessed King Jayavarman II's historic proclamation of Cambodian sovereignty in 802 AD, establishing the foundation of the mighty Angkor civilization.

Sacred Waterfall Picnic Experience

Savor authentic Cambodian cuisine with flame-grilled poultry, fragrant jasmine rice, and fresh tropical fruits while surrounded by the mystical cascades of Kulen Mountain. This enchanting al fresco meal transforms lunch into an unforgettable cultural encounter at Cambodia's holiest natural sanctuary.

Kulen Mountain Waterfall Swimming

Experience pure happiness under Kulen Mountain's cascading waters and enjoy the invigorating power of crystal-clear mountain cascades, where ancient sacred waters provide the perfect natural swimming experience.

Return Back to Your Hotel

Transfer you back to the Siem Reap downtown and it is possible to drop you off, either at your hotel or Old market area. You are supposed to be back to your hotel by 15:30.
