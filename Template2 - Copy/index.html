One-Day Angkor Wat Sunrise Tour Itinerary


4:30 AM - Sunrise at Angkor Wat


Arrive early at Angkor Wat to witness the stunning sunrise over the temple. The reflection in the surrounding moat creates a breathtaking view. Spend some time exploring the temple after sunrise, appreciating its intricate carvings and architecture.

7:30 AM - Breakfast
Enjoy breakfast at a nearby café or bring a packed meal to eat in the temple area.
8:30 AM - Ta Prohm Temple
Head to Ta Prohm, famous for its trees growing over the ruins. Take your time to explore this unique site, which has been left largely in its natural state.
10:00 AM - Ta Keo Temple
Visit Ta Keo, known for its steep staircases and unfinished towers. This less-crowded temple offers a different perspective on the Angkor complex.
11:30 AM - Bayon Temple
Arrive at Bayon Temple, known for its many serene and smiling faces carved into the stone. Spend time wandering through the temple's fascinating corridors and taking photos.
1:00 PM - Lunch
Take a break for lunch. There are several local restaurants near the temples where you can enjoy traditional Khmer cuisine.
2:00 PM - Return to Angkor Wat or Explore More Temples
If time permits, you can return to Angkor Wat for further exploration or visit additional nearby temples such as Banteay Kdei or Preah Khan.
3:30 PM - Conclusion of Tour
Begin your journey back to your accommodation or next destination. Tips: Transportation: Consider hiring a tuk-tuk or a guide for the day for convenience. Tickets: Ensure you have your Angkor Pass, which grants access to the temples. Hydration: Bring water to stay hydrated throughout the day. Dress Code: Wear appropriate clothing that covers shoulders and knees, as these are religious sites. This itinerary provides a comprehensive experience of the key highlights of the Angkor archaeological area, balancing exploration with time for reflection and photography. Enjoy your tour!
